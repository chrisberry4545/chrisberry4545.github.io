Throbbing Goat, Lecherous Yellow Eyed Penguin and Soggy Great White Shark. Animal Adjectives combines a random adjective with a random animal. You won't know how you've managed to live so long without it. Other features include:

- Push notifications with new animal adjectives
- Sharing to Facebook
- Favourites system
